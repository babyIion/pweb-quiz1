Pantai Selatan

There are several beautiful beaches on Malang's southern coast. Such as Teluk Asmoro, Watuleter, Sendang Biru, Goa China, Batubengkung, and Bajul Mati.

Waterfall

Many waterfalls can be found on Batu, Malang. For instance, there are Coban Kethak, Coban Pelangi, Coban Rondo, and Coban Nirwana. At these waterfalls, we can relax our body and enjoy the amazing view.

Hawai Waterpark

Hawai Waterpark is a waterpark which is located on Graha Kencana Utara V Street, Karanglo, Singosari, Malang. The most favourite or superior rides are Hawai Waterhouse and Waikiki Beach which is Tsunami Pool.
