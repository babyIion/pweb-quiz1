Orem-orem

Orem-orem is Malang's local food which contains tempe, chicken, lontong, and coconut milk. It usually served with urap-urap. Boiled egg is an additional ingredient. Orem-orem can be found on the side of the road, sold by streed vendors.

Bakso

One of the popular local food in Malang. It is not complete if we visited Malang and did not eat Bakso. Bakso is meatball with broth.

Angsle

Angsle is an appetizer. It contains bread cubes; traditional foods such as petulo, ketan putih, and mutiara; and two kinds of beans: mung bean and peanut. it is served with coconut milk.
